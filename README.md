📌 Project Overview

This project presents an interactive Power BI dashboard analyzing Airbnb performance across 10 major global cities. The objective is to transform raw listing and review data into actionable business insights for decision-makers.

The dashboard focuses on answering executive-level questions related to:

Market Share
Business Growth
Property Pricing
Customer Satisfaction
Seasonality
Host Trust & Verification
🎯 Business Problem

Airbnb generates millions of listings worldwide, making it difficult for stakeholders to monitor performance across cities.

This dashboard provides a centralized view to help answer questions such as:

Which cities contribute most to Airbnb's business?
How has Airbnb grown over time?
Which property types dominate the market?
Does pricing influence customer adoption?
Which cities deliver the best customer experience?
How trustworthy are Airbnb hosts?

📈 Key Insights
Paris, New York, and Sydney account for nearly half of Airbnb listings and approximately 59% of all reviews.
Airbnb experienced rapid growth until 2015 before slowing due to regulations and COVID-19.
Higher hotel prices may contribute to stronger Airbnb adoption in cities such as Paris.
Mexico City and Rio de Janeiro achieved the highest guest satisfaction scores.
Cleanliness and value for money remain common improvement areas.
Most guests leave reviews only once, suggesting many are infrequent reviewers.
